Hey! this game is for web 3 users, make sure to have metamask install. You can find here a quick demo of the gameplay while we add features and work on the tokenomics.



> READ COMMANDS

Move

Right Click to move at your cursor location

Competence 1:

Press "A" to throw projectiles at your enemies. Careful, you will loose munitions but no worries, you can grab some by running over the iron spawning across the same.

Competence 2:

Press "Z" to speed up.

Competence 3:

Press "E" to place a portal door. Press "E" a second time to place the other portal door.

Competence 4:

Press "R" to detonate an exploosion at your current location to destrooy ennemies.

Competence 5:

Press "D" to slow time a few seconds for your enemies.
